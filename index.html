Dashboard Guide

  • Challenge Status: Tracks balance, health score (last 10 days), and mental quiz accuracy.
  • Projections: Shows initial goal vs. your actual average vs. best-case Day 100 estimate.
  • Log Today: Pass the Prequiz first → check your rules → enter P&L → Complete Day.
  • Edit/Delete: Click ✏️ or 🗑️ on any row. Capital recalculates automatically across all days.
  • Cloud Sync: Sign in with Google (top right) to sync data across devices via Firebase.

About This Challenge

A focused accountability tool for crypto spot traders. Beyond P&L tracking — it enforces the psychological discipline that separates profitable traders from the rest.

Why 100 Days? Clear, achievable timeframe to master emotional control and compounding. The mandatory Prequiz and Rules checklist build the habits of profitable trading: focus, consistency, systematic execution.
