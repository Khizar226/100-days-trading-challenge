The 10 Golden Rules

Rules I follow before every single trade. No exceptions.

01.Confirm the trend before entering — EMA or price structure
02.Set stop-loss BEFORE placing the trade — not after
03.Risk ≤ 2% of current capital per trade
04.No trading in the first 15 minutes of market open
05.I am NOT emotionally compromised right now
06.Check major news and upcoming events before trading
07.Take-profit target is realistic — minimum 1:2 Risk:Reward
08.No revenge trading after a loss — ever
09.I will NOT move my stop-loss against myself
10.Yesterday's journal entry is complete before I trade today
